I’m excited to share my project: **AgriDeal AI - Smart Crop Market Negotiator**.

AgriDeal AI is a smart farming web app that helps farmers make better crop-selling decisions. The app supports price prediction, buyer negotiation, scam risk checking, storage recommendations, market alerts, and a farm-to-consumer selling mode where farmers can sell directly to homes, hotels, and restaurants.

## What the project solves

Many farmers face uncertain market prices, middlemen dependency, delayed payments, and limited access to trusted buyers. AgriDeal AI brings these decision points into one simple browser-based tool.

## Key features

- Crop, vegetable, fruit, and flower support
- Price prediction and profit analysis
- Buyer trust score and scam risk detection
- AI negotiation assistant
- Smart storage and selling timer
- Farm-to-consumer mode to cut middlemen
- Multilingual UI and voice support
- Auto-generated deal report

## Tech stack

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and browser Web Speech API.
